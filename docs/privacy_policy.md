# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2026

Welcome to KnoQ. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Personal Information:** Name, email, academy code.
- **Sensor Data:** We collect processed metric data from the KnoQ Smart Bat (power, swing, zone). We do not store raw IMU data on our servers.
- **Usage Data:** Device IDs, app interaction metrics.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- To provide coaching insights and analytics.
- To sync data between devices.
- To improve our sensor algorithms.

## 3. Data Deletion
You can permanently delete all your data and your account via the Settings screen in the KnoQ application.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use Firebase for authentication and database services. Your data is stored securely in Firebase and our PostgreSQL instance.

## 5. Contact Us
For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact support@knoq.in.
